## 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2022

### 🎯 Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze **Vrinda Store’s annual sales data** to understand customer behavior, sales performance, and order trends. The dashboard helps identify **top-performing regions, customer segments, and sales channels** to support data-driven decision making.

---

### 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers & Filters
- Data Cleaning and Formatting

---

### 📈 Dashboard Features
- Orders vs Sales monthly trend analysis
- Sales distribution by **Men vs Women**
- Order status analysis (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
- Top-performing states by sales
- Sales performance by channel (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)
- Interactive filters for dynamic analysis

---

### 🔍 Key Insights
- Women customers contributed a **higher percentage of total sales** compared to men.
- The majority of orders were **successfully delivered**, indicating strong operational efficiency.
- A few states contributed a **significant share of total revenue**, highlighting key markets.
- Online marketplaces such as **Amazon and Myntra** generated a large portion of sales.
- Monthly trends revealed **seasonal fluctuations in demand**, useful for planning inventory and promotions.

---

### 💡 Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Monitor sales performance easily
- Identify high-performing regions and channels
- Understand customer demographics
- Make better decisions in marketing and inventory planning

---

### 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how **Excel dashboards and visualization techniques** can convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that support business growth and strategic planning.
